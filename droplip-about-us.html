GET TO KNOW US!

Why we started droplip

DROPLIP was founded by Devasree and Sherlene in 2023.

United by a passion for sustainability and beauty, we wanted to address the environmental challenges while incoporating the ever-growing beauty industry. Disturbed by the excessive use of harmful chemicals and non-biodegradable packaging, we envisioned a cosmetic brand that would redefine beauty without compromising the planet.

In our pursuit, we meticulously curated biodegradable lipsticks that marries the allure of beauty with the responsibility of sustainability. We delved into the realms of cruelty-free practices and sought out natural, organic ingredients, ensuring that every product not only enhances your beauty but also respects the delicate balance of our environment.

Our commitment extends beyond the product formulation; we're on a mission to revolutionise packaging. Exploring innovative, eco-friendly materials, we aim to minimise waste and contribute to a circular economy. We believe that beauty shouldn't come at the expense of the planet, and every step we take is geared towards proving that sustainable practices can seamlessly coexist with glamour.

As our cosmetic brand gains momentum, we find fulfilment in the growing community that shares our values. It's not just about creating products; it's about fostering a movement where beauty becomes synonymous with ethical choices and environmental stewardship. HOW IT WORKS

It all starts with a lipstick. Our DROPLIPs are clear lipsticks that swatches with colours that forms right from your skin's pH level! Our clear lippies allow you to see a sneak of what seed your lipstick has! Plant seeds are embedded in our biodegradable product packaging that allows for easy disposal to harvest the growth of your little sapling! Simply use your Droplip to your desire and dispose of it on any form of soil (yes even the grass outside!) and with sufficient water and sunlight, your lippie will turn into a plant that contributes to more greenery around us. Meaning fresher air and better habitats for insect and animal species to live!

  • OUR MATERIALS

    We responsibly source our seeds from local certified organic farms and obtain biodegradable materials from reputable suppliers who adhere to strict environmental standards. Our commitment to sustainability extends to every step of our supply chain, ensuring transparency and ethical practices in sourcing materials for our products, for you!

  • WHAT IMPACT DO YOU MAKE FROM THIS?

    With every tube of Droplip purchased annually, you restore up to 20% of lost leafy nature! And the more hands on deck, the higher the impact! Your support not only enhances your beauty but also contributes to reforestation efforts, ensuring that we give back to the environment more than we take.
